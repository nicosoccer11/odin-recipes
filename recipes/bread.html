Garlic Bread

Description

Garlic Bread Bread topped with garlic and olive oil or butter and may include additional herbs

Ingredients

  • Italian/French Bread
  • Unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 Large garlic cloves
  • Parsley
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 350
  2. Cut Loaf in half and mix butter garlic parsley in a bowl
  3. Spread butter mixture over the bread
  4. Heat in oven for 10 minutes, sprinkle parmesan cheese on after
