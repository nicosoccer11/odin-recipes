Brownie

Description

Brownie A small square of rich chocolate cake, can contain nuts

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sugar/Salt
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Eggs
  • All purp flour

Steps

  1. Melt butter with sugar, salt, cocoa powder
  2. Add vanilla and eggs and beat
  3. Add flour and beat
  4. Bake in over (temp depends on what size)
